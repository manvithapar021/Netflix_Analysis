# Netflix Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes Netflix Movies and TV Shows data using Python, Pandas, and Matplotlib.

The objective is to identify trends in Netflix's content library and gain insights into content distribution, genres, ratings, countries, and growth patterns.

## Dataset
- Netflix Movies and TV Shows Dataset
- 8,807 titles
- 12 attributes

## Technologies Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Google Colab

## Analysis Performed

### 1. Movies vs TV Shows
- Compared the distribution of Movies and TV Shows on Netflix.

### 2. Top Content-Producing Countries
- Identified the countries contributing the most content.

### 3. Netflix Content Growth Over Time
- Analyzed content additions by year.

### 4. Most Popular Genres
- Determined the most common genres on Netflix.

### 5. Content Ratings Distribution
- Examined audience-targeting through ratings.

## Key Findings
- Movies account for approximately 70% of Netflix's catalog.
- The United States contributes the most content.
- Netflix experienced rapid growth between 2016 and 2019.
- International Movies and Dramas are the most common genres.
- Most content is targeted toward adults and teenagers.
